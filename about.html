About The Game

Space Invaders is an entertainment game that developed using HMTML5, CSS and JavaScript By FBI Script Team.

Chickens are invading our galaxy and it's your mission to pilot a spaceship to destroy them out and save the world.

You'll take on increasingly difficult waves of chickens and asteroids that threaten to destroy Earth.
