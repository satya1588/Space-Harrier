Instructions


THE GAME

The object of the game is, basically, to shoot the invaders with your laser base

while avoiding their shots and preventing an invasion.

Amassing a high score is a further objective and one that must be prioritized against your continued survival.


GAME LEVELS

The game conists of three levels, each level consists of two stages

Each level differ in number of invader, number of hits to destory the invader, the amount of missiles an invader can fire


HOW TO PLAY

Before playing you have to choose between of two laser bases

To move the laser base move use --> the right the left and right arrows

To fire a missile press --> space
